### What is unity

Unity is a game engine that supports 3D, 2D, VAR & AR.  
It also includes its own built in physics engine that's almost realistic.

<br><hr><br>
### Usage

Unity is mainly used to create video games, but it can also be used for simulations or any 3D application.

<br><hr><br>
### Features

Unity engine holds a lot of features, including and not limited to:  

1. Animations.
2. Two rendering pipelines. (URP & HDRP)


### Rendering

1. HDRP (High Definition Rendering Pipeline): Mainly used to render games with high textures & high polycounts.
2. URP (Universal Rendering Pipeline): less powerful than the HDRP and Mainly used to render games for mobile devices.

<br><hr><br>
### Support

Unity exports to multiple systems, including Windows, Mac, Android, IOS & Consoles.
